Project type:A Demo hub

A year Later Robert is now able to teach local fish farmers how to feed and look after Catfish.

Kigaya Village.

The contributions of incredible monthly donors and others giving directly to Thrive Uganda mission allow us to setup and facilitate such projects strengthening relationships with communities and evaluating them over time. The consistent visits allow us to learn vital lessons and  analyze the success of the projects.
The community members of Kigaya can now learn how to make bricks as well as how to farm fish as well as carpentry.
Building this demo hub is only one step towards training youth in employable and entrepreneurial  skills they can use to earn a living. Thrive Uganda Mission is committed to consistent monitoring of each project. Our monitoring and evaluation program, made possible by donors like you, allows us to maintain our relationships with communities by visiting up to 2 times each year to ensure that the projects are safe and reliable.
